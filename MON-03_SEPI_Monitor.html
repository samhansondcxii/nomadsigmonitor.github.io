Composite Methodology

SEPI = (SEI × 0.30) + (SPI × 0.30) + (SSP × 0.40)

Pillar sub-indicators are pre-normalized (min-max, defined ceiling benchmarks) and pre-calculated in the source workbook prior to ingestion. This dashboard consumes finished pillar and composite scores — no scoring logic is recomputed client-side.

Tier bands: TIER I ≥ 50 · TIER II 20–49.9 · TIER III < 20, applied against composite SEPI score.
